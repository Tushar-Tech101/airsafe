What is Air Pollution?

Harmful particles and gases in the air that damage health and environment.

PM2.5: Enters lungs & blood
PM10: Dust, pollen, mold
NO₂: From vehicles
Ozone: Smog gas

Major Sources

Vehicle Exhaust

40% of urban NO₂ & PM

Crop Burning

Delhi smog in Oct-Nov

Industries

Coal plants, brick kilns

Construction

Uncovered sites = dust cloud

Health Effects

Immediate

  • Coughing, eye irritation
  • Asthma attacks
  • Headache, fatigue

Long-term

  • Lung cancer
  • Heart disease
  • Reduced life by 5–10 years
